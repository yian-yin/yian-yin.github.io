About me

I am Yian Yin, a PhD candidate of Industrial Engineering & Management Sciences (IEMS) at Northwestern University. I am also a researcher in residence at Kellogg Center for Science of Science and Innovation (CSSI) and Northwestern Institute on Complex Systems (NICO), where I am fortunate to be co-advised by Prof. Dashun Wang and Prof. Noshir Contractor. I study computational social science, complex systems and data science, focusing on using interdisciplinary approaches to understand science and technology. My research has been published in general audience journals including Science, Nature, Nature Reviews Physics and Nature Human Behaviour and featured in many media outlets such as Forbes, Scientific American, Washington Post, The Atlantic, Harvard Business Review and MIT Technology Review.

I will be on the 2021-2022 academic job market and am looking for tenure-track positions.
