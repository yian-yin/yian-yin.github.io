About me

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate of Industrial Engineering & Management Sciences (IEMS) at McCormick School of Engineering, Northwestern University. I am also a researcher in residence at Center for Science of Science and Innovation and Northwestern Institute on Complex Systems (NICO), where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dashun Wang from Kellogg School of Management. Before joining Northwestern, I got my Bachelor degrees in Statistics (School of Mathematical Sciences) and Economics (National School of Development) from Peking University in 2016.
